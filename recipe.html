Shahi Biryani

Ingredients:

For the Rice: 2 cups of basmati rice, 4 cups of water, 1 bay leaf, 2-3 green cardamom pods, Salt to taste,
For the Shahi Biryani Masala: 1 cup of yogurt, 1 cup of fried onions, 1/2 cup of chopped mint leaves, 1/2 cup of chopped cilantro (coriander leaves), 1/4 cup of cashew nuts, fried until golden brown, 1/4 cup of raisins, 1/4 cup of ghee (clarified butter), A pinch of saffron strands soaked in 2 tablespoons of warm milk, Salt to taste.
For the Shahi Biryani Spice Blend: 1 cinnamon stick, 4-5 green cardamom pods, 4-5 cloves, 1 bay leaf, 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds.
For the Marinated Meat (Choose one): 500 grams of chicken, mutton, or vegetables (like potatoes and carrots for a vegetarian version) 1 cup of yogurt 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder 1 teaspoon of red chili powder Salt to taste

Procedure

Prepare the Rice: Wash the basmati rice thoroughly and soak it in water for 30 minutes. In a large pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. Add the soaked rice, bay leaf, green cardamom pods, and salt. Cook the rice until it's 70-80% done (al dente). Drain the water and set the rice aside.
Marinate the Meat or Vegetables: In a bowl, mix the meat or vegetables with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
Prepare the Shahi Biryani Masala: Heat ghee in a large, heavy-bottomed pot. Add the shahi biryani spice blend (cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, cloves, bay leaf, cumin seeds) and saut√© for a minute until aromatic. Add the marinated meat or vegetables and cook until they are partially cooked (for about 5-7 minutes).
Layer the Biryani: In the pot with the partially cooked meat or vegetables, add a layer of the parboiled rice. Sprinkle fried onions, chopped mint, chopped cilantro, cashew nuts, and raisins over the rice. Pour the saffron milk over the top for color and aroma. Repeat the layers until all the rice and garnishes are used.
Cook the Shahi Biryani: Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid or seal it with dough to trap steam. Cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes or until the meat or vegetables are tender and the rice is fully cooked. Be careful not to burn the bottom; you can use a heat diffuser if needed.
Serve: Gently fluff the Shahi Biryani with a fork before serving. Serve hot with raita (yogurt sauce) or a side salad.
